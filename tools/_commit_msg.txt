fix: flush speech queue on Back to prevent context-destroy crash (VP-SELECTCIV-BACK-CRASH-1)

When OnBack() is called (ESC or BackButton), DequeuePopup+SetHide destroy
the SelectCivilization Lua context. Any pending speech callbacks that hold
references to upvalues of the dead context can crash the engine.

Fix: hook the OnBack global so the speech queue is flushed before context
destruction. Also intercept VK_ESCAPE directly in the wrapped InputHandler
as a safety net for the keyboard path.

- hook OnBack global: pcall(speakInterrupt("")) then origOnBack()
- intercept VK_ESCAPE in InputHandler: flush then pass to origInput
- every pcall failure logged with Log.warn
- no existing logic removed
